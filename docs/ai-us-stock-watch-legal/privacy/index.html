Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AI美股观察 handles information when using its private content workflow and TikTok Content Posting API integration.

Information We Use

AI美股观察 may use the following information for the content publishing workflow:

  • Local video files selected by the channel owner for upload.
  • TikTok authorization tokens granted by the channel owner.
  • TikTok upload responses, such as upload status and publish identifiers.
  • Basic metadata prepared by the channel owner, such as draft captions or file names.

How Information Is Used

Information is used only to upload short-form stock analysis videos to the authorized TikTok account's draft or inbox flow and to support creator review before posting.

AI美股观察 does not use TikTok authorization to access unrelated user data, does not manage third-party accounts, and does not sell personal information.

Data Storage

Video files and workflow files may be stored locally by the channel owner. TikTok access tokens, if stored, should be kept in private local credentials or environment variables and should not be committed to public repositories.

Data Sharing

AI美股观察 sends selected video files and required upload metadata to TikTok only when the channel owner initiates an upload. Information is not sold or shared with advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Token Control

The authorized TikTok user can revoke access through TikTok account settings or the TikTok Developer authorization flow. Revoking access may prevent future draft uploads until authorization is granted again.

Security

Reasonable local safeguards should be used to protect access tokens and unpublished videos. No system can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

AI美股观察 is not intended for children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The effective date will be revised when material changes are made.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the channel owner through the contact method listed on the AI美股观察 channel or related public profile.
